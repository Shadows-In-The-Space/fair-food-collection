# Fair Food Collection — Stitch Design Prompt

## Concept
A vibrant food blog celebrating American State Fair traditions, Southern Soul Food, and fried fair food classics. The site captures the warmth of a summer night at the fair — carnival lights, the smell of frying oil, handwritten menu boards, and that unmistakable feeling of indulgence.

## Vibe
- **Carnival meets Southern comfort** — warm, nostalgic, indulgent
- **Hand-painted menu board aesthetic** — bold lettering, slightly rough edges
- **Golden hour at the fairground** — warm amber light, string lights, worn wood textures
- **Bold and unapologetic** — rich photography, saturated colors, no minimalist pretension

## Typography
- **Display/Headers:** "Abril Fatface" — bold, editorial, carnival poster feel
- **Subheadings:** "Josefin Sans" — slightly condensed, vintage signage
- **Body:** "Crimson Text" — warm, readable, editorial

## Layout
- **Hero:** Full-bleed food photography with warm overlay, bold headline, string light decoration
- **Recipe Grid:** Masonry-style cards with large images, category badges, cook time
- **Recipe Detail:** Magazine-style with sticky ingredients sidebar, large step images
- **Sections:** Checkered patterns as dividers, star/sparkle decorations, bunting accents

## Design Elements
- String lights SVG decorations (animated subtle glow)
- Star ratings and sparkle icons
- Checkered tablecloth pattern (subtle, as section dividers)
- Hand-drawn style borders and badges
- Bunting/banner decoration for headers
- Warm vignette overlays on images
- Badge ribbons for "New", "Favorite", "Spicy"

## Components
1. **Recipe Card** — Large food image (16:10), category tag, title, cook time, difficulty badge, star rating
2. **Category Pills** — Mustard gold rounded pills: "Frittiert", "Süß", "Soul Food", "Schnell", "Herzhaft"
3. **Hero Banner** — Full-width with string lights, overlay text, CTA button
4. **Recipe Detail View** — Image hero, ingredients list with checkboxes, step-by-step with images
5. **Event Calendar Card** — Date badge, event name, location, "Wir sind dabei" ribbon
6. **Newsletter Signup** — Warm background, simple form, golden button
7. **Footer** — Checkered pattern, social icons, warm brown background

## Pages
1. **Home** — Hero + Featured recipes + Categories + Latest posts + Newsletter
2. **Recipe Collection** — Filterable masonry grid of all recipes
3. **Recipe Detail** — Full recipe with ingredients, steps, tips, related recipes
4. **Blog/Stories** — Event recaps, market visits, food adventures
5. **Über Uns** — Our story, the fair food philosophy

## Content Examples
- Funnel Cakes, Corn Dogs, Fried Cheese, Fried Oreos, Loaded Fries, BBQ Pulled Pork
- Categories: Frittiert, Süß, Soul Food, Herzhaft, Schnell, Getränke
