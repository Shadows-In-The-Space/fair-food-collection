# Content Brief: Jahrmarkt Food — Kirmes & Karneval Rezepte

## Why this is the HIGHEST priority

Per the SEO-MASTER-PLAN:
- "GoldenCrumb is in the Ruhrgebiet — the heart of German Kirmes culture (Cranger Kirmes, größter Volksfest Europas)"
- "No blog bridges German Kirmes + American State Fair food"
- "This is a zero-competition content gap"

**Strategic insight:** Most recipe blogs are either German (covering German food) or American (covering American food). GoldenCrumb's position is the bridge. This pillar page establishes the bridge.

This is also the most natural DE-market entry point: most existing goldencrumb content is DE-targeted (per the Init-Commit, 7+ pages are already in German: `imbiss-food-rezepte.html`, `jahrmarkts-klassiker.html`, `wissenschaft-frittieren.html`).
