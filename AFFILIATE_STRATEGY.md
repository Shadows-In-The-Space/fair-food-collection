# AFFILIATE_STRATEGY.md — Golden Crumbs

## Overview
This document outlines the affiliate strategy for GoldenCrumb.de — the American Fair Food blog. We earn a small commission when readers click our Amazon links and make purchases. The price is the same for buyers, so there's no extra cost.

## Program
- **Network:** Amazon Associates (Amazon PartnerNet)
- **Associate Tag:** `goldencrumb0f-21` (placeholder — replace with real tag when approved)
- **Countries:** Primarily Germany (.de), also Austria (.at), Switzerland (.ch)

```

```

```

```

## Affiliate Section Placement
Added to ALL recipe HTML pages, BEFORE the `.related-recipes` section.

## Ad Banner Placement
Added to ALL pages (including index.html), in the footer area at the top of the footer section.
